In this previous article we have discussed about the DJI Mavic Air 2. Only the Fly More Combo version includes the original DJI Mavic Air 2 shoulder bag, which is very expensive in the case you want to buy it separately, so in this blog post we will take a look at a cheaper Mavic Air 2 bag which is very good for carrying the aircraft during travel: DJI Mavic Air 2 Drone Bag.

Bag exterior

The bag is available in two different colors: black and gray. In this review the black version is showed.

The bag comes also with a shoulder strap, in case you do not want to use the handle for carrying it:

The strap has to be mounted on using the dedicated holders:

This is how it appears with shoulder strap mounted on:

The handle is pretty comfortable.

Inside the bag

Opening the bag, we can see two sections: the top one can be used to carry spare parts, like propellers; the other one is designed to store the DJI Mavic Air 2, the remote controller, LiPo batteries (up to 6, plus the one on the drone itself) and all the accessories like charger and cables.

The compartments are very well designed and everything fits perfectly and secured:

Conclusions

If you are looking for a cheaper alternative to the original DJI shoulder bag for carrying your DJI Mavic Air 2, this is a valid choice. The quality of the material is pretty good and the storage space is very well designed for carrying everything you need when traveling with your drone.
